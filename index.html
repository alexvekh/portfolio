Mobile Bank App

Full-stack web application empowered users to perform a spectrum of tasks, including registration with code confirmation, login, logout, password changes, and the restoration of forgotten passwords. Authenticated users gained access to personal account balances, transaction history, and notifications, enabling them to simulate transactions between pay systems and transfer money to other accounts. Rigorous input form validations were applied throughout the application to ensure data integrity.
